Congressional App Challenge

Project Showoff Video

Cash Cattle is an app created by Daniel Schwarz and Dean Quiambao which is meant to teach teenagers the basics of stock trading. The app accomplishes this with multiple lessons that go over things like explaining what stocks are, how they work, terminology, and why trading stocks is a valuable skill. The app also has a practice section that gives the user real prompts from history that relate to the stock market along with an option to either invest or hold giving them an idea of how investing works.
